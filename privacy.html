Offline-first privacy for the LINA ICE app.

Overview

How the app works and what we handle.

LINA ICE ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. The app operates fully offline and does not send personal information to our servers.

What the app does

On-device features

  • Displays curated content about waterfront residences and services.
  • Lets you run local calculators and optional exports on-device.
  • Allows exporting PDFs/CSVs you generate, which you share using your own apps.
Data collection

What we do not collect

  • No analytics/ads SDKs and no tracking.
  • No server collection of personal data.
  • Optional contact actions stay on your device; you control any sharing.
Permissions

Secure by design

The app avoids dangerous permissions. Sharing uses Android's secure FileProvider to grant temporary, read-only access to files you choose.

Commitments

How we share, protect, and update.

Your data remains on your device. When you choose to export, you decide where it goes.

Sharing & transfers

Your exports, your choice

We do not sell or share data with third parties. No data leaves your device unless you export and share it yourself.

Children

Children's privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Updates

Policy changes

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. The latest version and effective date are posted on this page.
